Class: Chair

Attributes:
color (string)
chairType (string)
occupied (boolean)
outdoor (boolean)
pillows (array)
legs (integer)

Methods:
changeColor (changes the color attribute)
addPillow (add +1 pillows to the pillows array)
checkOccupancy (if occupied, occupied = true. if open, occupied = false)
weatherResistent (if yes, outdoor = true. if no, outdoor = false)
countLegs (adds number of legs on chair to legs attribute)
